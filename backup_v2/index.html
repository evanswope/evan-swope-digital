About Evan

I believe that the best creative solutions are backed by rigorous systems design, and the best business strategies are brought to life through beautiful visual storytelling.

Over my 15-year career, I have made 8 original brands from scratch, written their guidelines, scaled email campaigns, and worked in formats ranging from documentaries to scoring audio for contemporary dance and robotics.

Lately, my focus has shifted toward building operational systems that let companies scale. At Crushworthy, that meant setting up data-driven B2B lead generation lists and redesigning their online retail front-end. At Marx Companies, it meant designing AI-integrated workflows to increase media output 5x.

Beyond the desk: In my off time, you can find me tackling home improvement projects in my Seattle condo, doing stand-up comedy, or drag. I graduated from the University of Washington with a BFA in Digital Art & Experimental Media (DXARTS) and a BA in Communication.
